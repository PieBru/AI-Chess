🧩 What is chess, anyway?

Chess is a game for two players. You each have an army of pieces, and you take turns moving them across a checkered board. The big goal: trap the other player's King so it can't escape — that's called checkmate, and it wins the game!

👋 Meet your opponents

In this game, you can play against four totally different kinds of opponents. Here they are!

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

A Friend

Play against a real person sitting next to you. Take turns clicking moves!

🤖

Practice Bot

A computer friend with 5 levels, from "still learning" to "quite tricky." Pick one that's just right for you.

🧙

Grandmaster Gustav

The super-genius. Gustav almost never makes a mistake — a fun (and tough!) challenge.

💬

Chatty the AI Pen Pal

A grown-up can hook up a talking AI to play a side too — it picks its moves by thinking in words!

🎮 How to play in 4 easy steps

1

Pick who plays White and who plays Black — you, a Practice Bot, Gustav, or Chatty.

2

Press Start Game.

3

Click a piece to pick it up, then click a glowing square to put it down.

4

Take turns until someone wins, or the game ends in a draw. Then play again!

📊 The Smart-o-Meter

See that tall skinny bar next to the board? That's the Smart-o-Meter. When it fills up light, White is doing great. When it fills up dark, Black is ahead. Watch it change after every move!

🏷️ Move badges — how good was that move?

After a computer moves, you'll see a little badge telling you how smart the move was:

🌟Best — the smartest move possible!
👍Good — a solid, sensible move.
🤔Inaccuracy — hmm, there was a better choice.
😬Mistake — oops, that one costs something.
💥Blunder — whoa, big oopsie!

It's a fun way to spot which opponent is really the smartest one!

💡 Three tips from Nico the Knight

Tip 1: In the beginning, try to move your Knights and Bishops out early — they like to be in the game!
Tip 2: Keep your King safe. Tucking it behind some pawns (that's called castling) is usually smart.
Tip 3: Before you click a square, take a breath and look — is any of your pieces about to get captured?
